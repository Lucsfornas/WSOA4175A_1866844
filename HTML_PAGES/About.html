About Me


Hi, I am Luca Fornasari, a fourth year (honors) Digital Arts Student studying at the University of Witwaterstrand.


This is my Project website for fourth year Interactive Media, WSOA4175A. This project is focused on art on internet art. This form of art looks at the browser as the medium of the artwork while the code is comparable to the classical paintbrush.
